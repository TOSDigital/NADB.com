About Us

NADB

Welcome to the National Anti Duplication Bureau (NADB) - Your Trusted Guardians of Authenticity. NADB is dedicated to eradicating product duplication and counterfeiting, preserving the integrity of markets, and empowering local and indigenous businesses. We tirelessly protect intellectual property rights, promote innovation, and ensure consumers make informed choices. Explore our mission, join the fight, and be a part of a world where authenticity reigns supreme.

As a beacon of integrity in the marketplace, NADB leverages a potent blend of vigilant surveillance, policy advocacy, and strategic collaboration with law enforcement agencies. We offer support and resources to businesses to help them thrive in a competitive environment while advocating for stronger regulations to deter duplication and counterfeiting. At NADB, we envision a future where local products retain their value, where innovation flourishes, and where consumers can trust the authenticity of their purchases. Join us in this vital mission to protect the market and empower businesses – together, we'll build a more honest and vibrant commercial landscape.

Mission

To safeguard innovation, protect intellectual property rights, and foster a fair and competitive marketplace by relentlessly combating product duplication and counterfeiting. We strive to empower local and indigenous businesses, ensuring their products retain value in the market while educating consumers about the perils of counterfeit goods.

vision

To be a global leader in the fight against product duplication and counterfeiting, driving economic growth and innovation. We envision a world where local and indigenous products thrive, consumers make informed choices, and intellectual property rights are respected, ensuring a level playing field for all businesses while preserving the integrity of markets.

Our Services

Intellectual Property Protection

NADB can assist businesses in registering and protecting their intellectual property rights, such as patents, trademarks, and copyrights. This service ensures that their innovations are safeguarded from duplication and counterfeiting.

Counterfeit Detection &
  • Investigation

    • NADB can conduct investigations to identify counterfeit products in the market. This service helps businesses take legal action against those responsible for product duplication and counterfeiting.

    Marketplace Integrity Advocacy

    NADB can advocate for stronger laws and regulations aimed at deterring product duplication and counterfeiting. This service involves working with policymakers to create a more robust legal framework for intellectual property protection.
